### Medium priority

3. **Add 1–2 exact-phrase occurrences.** The exact phrase "private AI infrastructure for healthcare" appears only 3 times. Naturally inserting it once in the Phase roadmap section and once in the TCO section would strengthen exact-phrase signals without over-optimization.

4. **Add one internal link in the Phase roadmap section.** The roadmap is the longest section (≈700 words) and has no internal link. A contextual link to a relevant OneSource page (e.g., the healthcare page) in Phase 3 or Phase 5 would distribute link equity more evenly.

### Low priority
