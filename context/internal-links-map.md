## 1. Blog Auto Internal Linking Rules (Claude-Ready)

### Rules

Every blog post MUST include:

1. **1 Core Page Link**
   - https://onesourcecloud.net/private-ai-infrastructure  

2. **1 Feature Page Link**
   - https://onesourcecloud.net/ai-storage-architecture  
   OR  
   - https://onesourcecloud.net/high-performance-ai-networking  

3. **1 Industry Page Link**
   - healthcare / fintech / saas / research  

---

### Anchor Strategy

- Do NOT repeat exact same anchor text
- Mix:
  - "private AI infrastructure"
  - "enterprise AI infrastructure"
  - "dedicated AI environment"

---

### Placement Rules

- 1 link in first 2 paragraphs  
- 1 link mid-content  
- 1 link near CTA  

---

### Example (Claude Output Pattern)

> Enterprises are increasingly moving toward **private AI infrastructure** to ensure control and compliance.

> This becomes critical in environments like **AI infrastructure for healthcare**, where data sensitivity is high.
