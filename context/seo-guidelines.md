# OneSource Cloud – SEO Guidelines

## Keyword Strategy

### Primary Keyword Rules
- Include primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, 2+ H2s, meta title, meta description
- Target density: 1–2% (avoid stuffing)
- Use exact match and semantic variations naturally

### Secondary Keywords
- Integrate 3–5 secondary keywords throughout body
- Use in H2/H3 subheadings where natural
- Include in image alt text where relevant

---

## Content Structure

### Required Elements
1. **H1**: Primary keyword, sentence case, under 60 chars for SERP
2. **Introduction**: Hook + primary keyword in first 100 words
3. **Key Takeaways block**: After intro, before first H2
4. **Table of contents**: For articles over 2,000 words
5. **FAQ section**: 4–6 questions in natural language
6. **Conclusion**: Summary + CTA

### Heading Hierarchy
- H1: One per article (primary keyword)
- H2: Main sections (4–7 per article)
- H3: Subsections within H2s
- No H4+ unless absolutely necessary

### Paragraph Rules
- 2–4 sentences per paragraph
- One idea per paragraph
- 150–300 words per section

---

## Meta Elements

### Title Tag
- 50–60 characters
- Primary keyword near the front
- Include year for evergreen content
- Format: [Primary Keyword] – [Benefit/Year] | OneSource Cloud

### Meta Description
- 150–160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Directly answer the query (not just a teaser)
- Clear value proposition

### URL Slug
- Lowercase, hyphenated
- Primary keyword only
- No stop words (the, a, an, for)
- Format: /blog/primary-keyword-phrase

---

## Internal Linking

### Required Links (every article)
1. Core page: /private-ai-infrastructure
2. Feature page: /ai-storage-architecture OR /high-performance-ai-networking
3. Industry page: /ai-for-healthcare OR /ai-for-fintech OR /ai-for-saas

### Placement Rules
- First link in first 2 paragraphs
- One link mid-article
- One link near CTA/conclusion
- Use descriptive anchor text (no "click here")
- Vary anchor text across articles

---

## External Linking

### Requirements
- 2–3 external links per article
- Link to authoritative sources only (HHS, NIST, HITRUST, IEEE, major research)
- Open in same tab (Webflow handles this)
- Use descriptive anchor text

---

## AI Search Optimization

### Direct Answer First
- First 1–2 sentences must directly answer the target query
- AI scrapers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) pull from top of page

### Structured Data Signals
- FAQ section with natural-language questions
- Key Takeaways block with 3–5 specific bullet points
- One idea per section (clean signal for AI indexing)
- Author attribution in frontmatter

---

## Readability Standards

- Average sentence length: under 20 words
- Reading level: Grade 8–10 (Flesch 60–70)
- Active voice predominant
- Transition words between sections
- Short paragraphs (2–4 sentences max)
- Subheadings every 300–400 words

---

## Word Count Targets

| Content Type | Target |
|---|---|
| Pillar/cluster article | 2,500–3,500 words |
| Standard blog post | 2,000–2,800 words |
| FAQ/quick guide | 1,200–1,800 words |

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in 2+ H2 headings
- [ ] Keyword density 1–2%
- [ ] 3 internal links (core, feature, industry)

Every article must rank, convert, and build trust. SEO without conversion is traffic without revenue.
