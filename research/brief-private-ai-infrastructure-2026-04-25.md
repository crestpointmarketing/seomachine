## 2. Competitive Landscape

### Content Gaps (Opportunities)
1. **TCO comparison** — no real total cost of ownership breakdown vs. cloud
2. **Decision framework** — when to choose private vs. cloud vs. hybrid
3. **Security & compliance specifics** — HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2 context is surface-level everywhere
4. **Mid-market angle** — most content targets Fortune 500 or early startups
5. **Implementation roadmap** — step-by-step "how to actually do it" content is rare

### Differentiation Strategy
Lead with a practical decision framework + TCO logic. Target companies past "should we use AI" and into "how do we run it ourselves."
