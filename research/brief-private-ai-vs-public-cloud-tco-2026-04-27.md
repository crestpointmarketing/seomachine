## 2. Competitive Landscape

### Common Sections Across Top-Ranking Articles
- Upfront hardware costs vs. subscription pricing
- GPU compute pricing tiers
- Data egress and transfer fees
- Security and compliance overhead
- Operational staffing costs
- Break-even timelines

### Content Gaps (Opportunities)
- **Managed private AI angle**: Most articles assume self-managed on-prem. Fully managed private AI removes the operational burden argument.
- **Compliance cost quantification**: Regulated industry cost savings (HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR) rarely quantified.
- **Predictable cost as competitive advantage**: Public cloud pricing volatility rarely framed from a CFO risk perspective.
- **Performance-per-dollar at scale**: GPU spot instance interruptions create hidden productivity costs not captured in sticker price.
- **OneSource angle**: Fully managed lifecycle (Build → Operate → Orchestrate → Scale) eliminates the staffing objection.

### Differentiation Strategy
Frame TCO not as a pure cost analysis but as a **risk-adjusted return on infrastructure**. Public cloud hides cost in egress fees, compliance gaps, spot interruptions, and shared-resource variability. Private AI infrastructure—especially fully managed—delivers **predictable cost, predictable performance, and predictable compliance**.
