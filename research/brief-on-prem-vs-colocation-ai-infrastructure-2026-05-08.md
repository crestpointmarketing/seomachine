## 4. Supporting Elements

### Statistics & Data Points
- AI rack power density typically 40–100 kW vs traditional 5–10 kW
- Average enterprise data center build: 18–36 months
- Colocation deployment: 4–12 weeks for AI-ready capacity
- GPU refresh cycle: 24–36 months for leading-edge AI hardware
- Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE): purpose-built AI colos average 1.2–1.4 vs older enterprise DC at 1.8–2.0
- Cooling: liquid cooling now standard at >50 kW/rack densities
